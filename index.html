Greetings from IEEE-SJCE!!
Building a computer is fun, affordable and empowering. It's fun being able to choose all of the parts that go into your machine, and it's just so darn cool to DIY! It's time to gear up your computer jargon as the EDS wing of IEEE-SJCE in association with the Department of ECE, JSS STU brings forth, "WORKSHOP ON PC ASSEMBLY"
Right from putting the core components together to the necessary underlying concepts, we've got you covered in the journey of building a PC from scratch. You will go from having no idea what's inside a computer to having a full working computer with required OS and software .

SQL BOOTCAMP

Hey Engineers!
Greetings from IEEE-SJCE!!
BA 3 - day workshop wherein you can learn the basics of SQL - Structured Query Language, a conventional language that is used to communicate with the database. It has a wide range of applications in today's world especially in data science tools and with minimal code, you can do absolute wonders. There is also a hands-on project segment, where you will be building a solid backend component for the given frontend. Oh, by the way, the question at the top is widely asked across multiple companies like Amazon,Informatica etc. in their interviews. By the time you are done with our BootCamp, you will be confident enough to crack such questions with poise and ease. .

Dates: 30th Registration is free!

Platform: Google Meet
Date: 22nd, 23rd & 24th January 2022
